## A. 区块链

有

$$
\frac{1}{n} = \frac{1}{a \oplus b} + \frac{1}{b} \iff \frac{1}{n} = \frac{(a \oplus b) + b}{(a \oplus b)b} \iff (a \oplus b)b = ((a \oplus b) + b)n \iff a \oplus b = \frac{nb}{b - n}
$$

枚举 $b$ 计算取 $\max$ 即可．
